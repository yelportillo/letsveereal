About letsveereal

Behind the lens

Hello, I’m Viriel (“Yel”) — the photographer behind letsveereal photography.

The name letsveereal is inspired by my own name, Viriel, and reflects my approach to photography — capturing honest moments, natural light, and images that feel genuine.

I shoot with an Olympus OM-D E-M10 Mark IV, and every image is carefully refined in Adobe Lightroom for clean, timeless color and consistency. Same-day instant prints are available, so you leave with something real in your hands.

I offer mini sessions and custom shoots with flexible locations. Clients who can assist with transportation receive a 20% discount — keeping photography accessible without compromising quality.
