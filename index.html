Behind The Lens

A little about the person behind the camera.

Hello, I’m Viriel (“Yel”), the photographer behind letsveereal photography.

The name letsveereal is inspired by my own name, Viriel, and reflects my approach to photography—capturing honest moments, natural light, and images that feel genuine. My work focuses on authentic expressions and clean, timeless visuals that tell your story in a meaningful way.

I shoot with an Olympus OM-D E-M10 Mark IV paired with carefully selected lenses, and every image is professionally edited using Adobe Lightroom to ensure consistent color, clarity, and quality. I also offer same-day instant photo prints, allowing clients to leave their session with a physical memory the very same day.

I provide mini sessions and custom shoots with flexible locations tailored to your needs. For clients who can assist with transportation to their preferred location, a 20% discount is available—helping keep professional photography accessible while maintaining high-quality results.
